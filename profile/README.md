### [Gods of Sand](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1431230/Gods_of_Sand/)

**Genre:** Gladiator Manager / Turn-Based Strategy / Simulation / RPG

*Gods of Sand* is a gladiator manager with intense turn-based combat, heavily inspired by the classic *Swords and Sandals* series but set in a darker, more unforgiving world. You are tasked with restoring the glory of an abandoned *ludus* (gladiator school). Train your fighters, manage the economics of your business, and fight to become the champion of the arenas.

**Key Features:**
* **Manage Your Ludus:** Keep a close eye on your finances. Fight for prize money, train your gladiators to ensure a steady income, and rebuild your family's legacy.

### [Relic Dudes](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2116260/Relic_Dudes/)

**Genre:** Action Roguelike / Bullet Hell / Online Co-op

*Relic Dudes* is a fast-paced, "survivors-style" roguelite where you defeat hordes of enemies alone or with friends. Create unique builds that synergize with your team's powers and face off against massive waves of monsters and bosses.

**Key Features:**
* **Online Multiplayer:** Play solo or team up with up to 4 friends in online co-op mode. Work together or embrace the chaos.
* **Build Synergy:** Collect relics and upgrades to create powerful combinations that can change the fate of your run.
* **Massive Content:** Discover over 30 unique relics, 10 passive upgrades, and multiple unlockable characters.
* **Challenging Stages:** Fight through multiple diverse stages and conquer challenging boss encounters.
